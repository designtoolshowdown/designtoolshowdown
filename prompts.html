Use AI to Find Your Perfect Design Tool

Not sure which design tool fits your needs? These prompts help you get personalized recommendations from ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, or any other AI-enabled browser or search engine by asking the right questions.

Each prompt is designed to surface the trade-offs between the tools covered on this site‚Äîplus others you might not know about. Simply copy, customize with your specifics, and paste into your preferred AI chat.

üí° How to Use These Prompts

  1. Copy the prompt that matches your project type
  2. Replace the [bracketed sections] with your specific details
  3. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, or any AI-enabled search engine
  4. Ask follow-up questions if the answer isn't clear:
    • "Can you compare the pricing in detail?"

    • "Show me a step-by-step workflow for this"
Pro Tip: The more specific you are about budget, timeline, skill level, and commercial licensing needs, the better the AI recommendation will be.
PROMPT 1

Logo Design

"I need to design a logo for my [business type]. Style preference: [text-based / icon+text / illustrative]. Design experience: [beginner / intermediate / advanced]. Budget: $[amount]/month. Need commercial licensing: [yes / no]. Compare these tools: Canva, Kittl, Adobe Firefly, Midjourney. Which is best for my use case and why? Give me a specific 3-step workflow. Please recommend any other platforms or products I should consider."
PROMPT 2

Social Media Content at Scale

"I need to create [Instagram posts / LinkedIn graphics / Stories / Reels thumbnails] consistently‚Äîat least [number] per week. Team size: [solo / 2-3 people / larger team]. Design skills: [beginner / intermediate / advanced]. Budget: $[amount]/month. Compare Canva vs Adobe Firefly vs ChatGPT for this workflow. Consider: template libraries, batch creation, team collaboration, and brand consistency. Please recommend any other platforms or products I should consider."
PROMPT 3

Print-on-Demand / Merchandise

"I want to create designs for [t-shirts / mugs / posters / stickers] to sell on [Etsy / Shopify / Amazon Merch / Redbubble]. Design style: [typography-focused / illustrative / photographic]. Experience level: [beginner / intermediate / advanced]. Budget: $[amount]/month. Key needs: commercial licensing safety, high-resolution exports, typography tools. Compare Kittl, Canva, Adobe Firefly, and Midjourney for this use case. Please recommend any other platforms or products I should consider."
PROMPT 4

Multi-Tool Workflow Strategy

"I have a budget of $[amount]/month and need to build a complete design workflow for [describe your use case]. Should I use one tool for everything or combine multiple tools? For example: Should I use Midjourney for AI images + Canva for layouts? Or Adobe Firefly + Illustrator? Or ChatGPT + Kittl? Or just stick with Canva for everything? Compare single-tool vs multi-tool approaches. What's the best tool stack for my specific needs and budget? Please recommend any other platforms or products I should consider."
PROMPT 5

AI Image Generation (Style-Specific)

"I want to generate [photorealistic / artistic / abstract / illustrated] images for [specific purpose - e.g., website hero images, social content, product mockups]. Volume needed: [a few images / dozens per week / hundreds per month]. Quality vs speed preference: [highest quality / fast iterations / balanced]. Budget: $[amount]/month. Commercial use required: [yes / no]. Compare ChatGPT, Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, Grok, and Stable Diffusion for this. Consider: image quality, generation speed, prompt control, and licensing requirements. Please recommend any other platforms or products I should consider."
